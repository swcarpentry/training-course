1. Suppose that \`pwd\` prints \`/users/backup\`. Which of the following commands will navigate you to \`/users/thing/backup/\`?

a) ../../users/thing/backup  
b) cd ././users/thing/backup  
c) cd ../users/thing/backup  
d) cd ../../users/thing/backup
